Welcome to Bassinga!

Unlock the Fretboard

Bassinga is a practice tool designed to help you learn how to improvise on the bass guitar.

Stop playing the same songs and riffs from memory. Instead, choose a scale and key, explore the fretboard, and build real musical awareness.

Bassinga includes a built-in tuner that shows which notes you're playing in real time. This way you can have your cheat sheet and your tuner on the same screen while playing. Bassinga uses the microphone of your own computer, phone or any device.
The goal is simple:
stop relying on your eyes and start using your ears.

We recommend starting with the most commonly used scales:
- Major scale (pop, rock, funk, soul, and jazz)
- Minor pentatonic (rock, funk, and blues)

These scales form the foundation of countless bass lines and solos.

Need a quick refresher on music theory?
👉 bassguitarscales.org
